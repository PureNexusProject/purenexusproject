Welcome to the Pure Nexus Project.

The Pure Nexus Project aims to bring stability and features to your Nexus/Pixel device, while keeping Google's vision in line and not straying too far away from that. If you want a stable ROM with true tested features and minimal bugs, this is the place to be.
